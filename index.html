About Rimuru

Rimuru Tempest ｢リムル＝テンペスト, rimuru tenpesuto｣ is the main protagonist of That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime and the reincarnation of Satoru Mikami.

Quickly gaining a multitude of skills while assuming a human form, he becomes the founder and King of The Jura Tempest Federation and he resides in Capital City Rimuru.

​He is known as the "Tempest of the Great Jura Forest" due to the overwhelming impact he has on everything and everyone around him.

Rimuru's kindness and desire to be faithful to his old species doesn't bode well for his future, however he is willing to shoulder and take responsibility for any mistakes he makes along the way.

Latest News on That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime Season 3


When the third season was first revealed in November 2022, not much was known about it. The third season of That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime will premiere in the spring of 2024, according to information that was made public on February 22, 2023. The first announcement of this information came during a franchise celebration marking its tenth anniversary, during which the movie That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime: Scarlet Bond was shown for the last time in theaters. A revised release schedule, with an April 2024 premiere date, was unveiled in a new trailer in December 2023. There are rumors that the season will be divided into two courses, or halves.
